Why chose Savvy Life for your home? We take an approach to the design of your systems that starts with you, how you live in your home and what is important to you. We are not focused on specific products or solutions, rather, how how do we tailor a system to benefit you in your home and work with your life. Our goal is build a relationship for life with all of our clients, and that is only done by delivering what is right for each client individually.

What We Do

Savvy Life designs and installs all manner of home systems which help our customers enjoy their home environment to the fullest. These systems include Whole Home Audio/Video, Home Theaters, Networking, Lighting Control, Shade solutions, temperature control, and security. Using each of these systems we are able to provide the maximum benefit to our customers and their individual needs.

Savvy Life works with the builders of new homes to ensure that the infrastructure for the designed systems are installed correctly as the house is built eliminating the need to cut drywall retrofit the needed wiring. We will be there every step of the way working with the builder of your new home to install the system we designed with you.

Whole Home Audio/Video

Whole Home Audio/Video Systems from Savvy life will allow each person in the home to tune their environment to their personal tastes no matter what room they are in. You can choose the music or movie you want to watch in one room while a family member can choose their own music or TV show in another.

Savvy Life will work with you to determine what media services are relevant to you and ensure your system is designed to accommodate them and then make sure they are setup and optimized for your system. Also with your Whole Home Audio/Video system you will receive the ability to control your system from a hand held remote, in wall touch panel, or smart device optimized to ensure ease of use.

Home Theaters

Savvy Life Home Home Theaters are designed with you in mind. Whether you want your home theater to be a dedicated room that will offer the opulence of a Movie Theater reminiscent of the 1940’s, or a media room that is incorporated into a Rec Room or Man Cave, Savvy Life will take in to account all of the factors needed to make your Theater something truly special.

Networking

Savvy Life will install and configure your home network with the reliability of a commercial network. In todays day and age the network is the backbone of any home. With the extent of smart phones and tablets combined with laptops, smart TV’s and every other device that can be connected to the internet, it is crucial that every home has a network that can handle the workload.

When Savvy Life installs the network with the other systems in the home we can ensure that our systems will work together saving headaches down the road.

Lighting Control

With a Savvy Life Lighting Control System you can set the mood throughout the entire house for a party or just a normal night at home with the push of one button. Lighting control allows you to set ambiance, enjoy added security, experience energy savings and schedule daily routines so that the worry of turning lights on and off is eliminated.

When done right, the features of lighting control truly make life easier. Savvy Life works closely with our customers to determine how they live in their house to set the lighting control in a way that truly enhances the environment at home.

Shade Solutions

Imagine being able to open or close all of the shades in a single room or throughout the entire house at the push of a button. With Shade Solutions this is possible. And with other systems installed by Savvy Life, lighting, temperature, and shades can all be set at once.

Savvy Life installed and controlled Shade Solutions will work with the other systems in the house to enhance the home environment and make your home more comfortable. We will work with the interior design of the home to make sure the window coverings match the decor and theme of the home as well as give the correct functionality that the customer is looking for.

Temperature Control

A home that is too warm or too cold is uncomfortable. With a Savvy Life installed temperature control system you can set the temperature throughout your home from your smart phone, tablet, handheld remote, or in-wall touch panel. With other installed systems, the temperature control can be combined with shade solutions and lighting control to make the home comfortable to be in and energy efficient.

Security

Through security and camera systems your family can sleep soundly knowing that your home is protected. With camera DVR’s you can check on your home from anywhere in the world to ensure that your home is as you left it.
